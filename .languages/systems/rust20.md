# Top 20 Most Impactful Rust Developers

A ranked list of the most influential and expert Rust developers, with links and descriptions of their contributions.

---

## 1. [David Tolnay (dtolnay)](https://github.com/dtolnay)
- Core Library team member.
- Maintains foundational crates: **serde**, **syn**, **quote**, **thiserror**, **anyhow**, **proc-macro2**.
- Shapes std APIs and error handling idioms.

## 2. [Graydon Hoare](https://graydon2.dreamwidth.org/)
- Creator of Rust at Mozilla.
- Set early vision and direction for the language.

## 3. [Niko Matsakis](https://github.com/nikomatsakis)
- Rust Core team, type system design.
- Works on trait solving engine **Chalk**.
- Extensive blogs on Rust internals.

## 4. [Alex Crichton](https://github.com/alexcrichton)
- Maintainer of **Cargo**, **rustup**, **crates.io**.
- Major contributor to std/lib and build tooling.

## 5. [Carl Lerche](https://github.com/carllerche)
- Creator and lead of **Tokio** async runtime.
- Core for Rust’s async ecosystem.

## 6. [Sean McArthur](https://github.com/seanmonstar)
- Author of **hyper**, **h2**, and **warp**.
- Core networking layer for HTTP in Rust.

## 7. [Andrew Gallant (BurntSushi)](https://github.com/BurntSushi)
- Author of **ripgrep**, **regex** crate, **csv** crate.
- Known for high-performance developer tools.

## 8. [Alexey Kladov (matklad)](https://github.com/matklad)
- Originator of **rust-analyzer** (IDE/LSP).
- Influences daily developer productivity.

## 9. [Mara Bos (m-ou-se)](https://github.com/m-ou-se)
- Rust Library team lead.
- Author of *Rust Atomics and Locks*.
- Concurrency and std contributions.

## 10. [Ralf Jung](https://github.com/RalfJung)
- Maintains **Miri** interpreter.
- Research on **RustBelt** project, unsafe semantics.

## 11. [Amanieu d’Antras](https://github.com/Amanieu)
- Author of **parking_lot** (fast locks).
- Library team contributor.

## 12. [Stjepan Glavina (stjepang)](https://github.com/stjepang)
- Creator of **crossbeam** concurrency library.
- Contributed to async runtimes and channels.

## 13. [Simon Sapin](https://github.com/SimonSapin)
- Maintainer of **html5ever**, **cssparser**.
- Foundational work in Servo/web stack.

## 14. [Eliza Weisman (hawkw)](https://github.com/hawkw)
- Creator of **tracing** observability framework.
- Contributor to **Tokio** and async ecosystem.

## 15. [Josh Triplett](https://github.com/joshtriplett)
- Long-time language, std, and Cargo contributor.
- Active in RFCs and Rust governance.

## 16. [Steve Klabnik](https://github.com/steveklabnik)
- Co-author of *The Rust Programming Language* (“The Book”).
- Influential in Rust docs and education.

## 17. [Carol Nichols (carols10cents)](https://github.com/carols10cents)
- Co-author of *The Book*.
- Creator of **Rustlings** exercises.

## 18. [Jon Gjengset](https://github.com/jonhoo)
- Author of *Rust for Rustaceans*.
- Creator of **Crust of Rust** educational video series.

## 19. [David Hewitt](https://github.com/davidhewitt)
- Core maintainer of **PyO3** (Python↔Rust interop).
- Bridges Rust into the Python ecosystem.

## 20. [Manish Goregaokar](https://github.com/Manishearth)
- Rust Devtools/Clippy team.
- Extensive technical blogging and teaching on Rust internals.
