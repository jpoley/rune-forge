# Essential C++ Podcasts and Audio Resources

Comprehensive collection of podcasts, audio content, and regularly updated audio resources for C++ developers.

## Dedicated C++ Podcasts

### 1. CppCast
**Hosts**: Rob Irving and Jason Turner
**URL**: https://cppcast.com/
**Frequency**: Weekly
**Duration**: 45-60 minutes

**Why Essential**:
- Premier C++ podcast with industry expert guests
- Regular interviews with C++ committee members
- Coverage of latest C++ standards and features
- Community news and event coverage

**Notable Episodes**:
- Interviews with Bjarne Stroustrup (C++ creator)
- Herb Sutter on C++ standardization
- Scott Meyers on Effective C++ principles
- Kate Gregory on modern C++ adoption

**Regular Segments**:
- News roundup and community updates
- Tool and library spotlights
- Conference coverage and speaker interviews
- Community project showcases
