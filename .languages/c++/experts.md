# Top 15 C++ Experts to Follow

Current active experts in the C++ community who shape the language, provide valuable insights, and contribute significantly to the ecosystem.

## 1. Bjarne Stroustrup
**Role**: Creator of C++, Professor at Columbia University
**Key Contributions**:
- Original creator and designer of C++
- Author of "The C++ Programming Language"
- Continues to guide C++ evolution and standardization
- Champion of C++ Core Guidelines

**Areas of Expertise**: Language design, performance, systems programming, teaching
**Follow**:
- Website: http://www.stroustrup.com/
- Talks: CppCon, Going Native
- Books: "The C++ Programming Language", "Programming: Principles and Practice"
