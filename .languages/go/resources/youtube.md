## How to Find Quality Go Content

### Search Strategies
1. **Conference Names**: "GopherCon", "dotGo", "Go meetup"
2. **Expert Names**: Search for talks by Go team members
3. **Specific Topics**: "Go concurrency", "Go web development"
4. **Recent Content**: Filter by upload date for current practices

### Quality Indicators
- **Speaker Credentials**: Go team members, known experts
- **Conference Source**: Official conference channels
- **View Count**: Popular content often indicates quality
- **Comments**: Community engagement and positive feedback
- **Date**: Prefer recent content for current best practices

### Red Flags to Avoid
- **Outdated Content**: Pre-Go 1.11 modules content
- **Low Production Value**: Poor audio/video quality
- **Incorrect Information**: Verify against official documentation
- **Clickbait Titles**: Overly sensationalized content
