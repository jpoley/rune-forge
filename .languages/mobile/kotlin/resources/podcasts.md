## Kotlin-Focused Podcasts

### Talking Kotlin
- **URL**: https://talkingkotlin.com/
- **Host**: Hadi Hariri (JetBrains)
- **Format**: Interview-based
- **Episode Length**: 30-60 minutes
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly
- **Platform Availability**: Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, RSS
- **RSS Feed**: https://feeds.soundcloud.com/users/soundcloud:users:280353173/sounds.rss
- **Focus Areas**:
  - Kotlin language development
  - Community interviews
  - Multiplatform development
  - Industry trends and adoption
- **Notable Episodes**:
  - "The Story of Kotlin" with Andrey Breslav
  - "Kotlin Multiplatform Mobile" with Kevin Galligan
  - "Compose Multiplatform" with Sebastian Aigner
  - "Kotlin in Production" with various industry leaders
- **Target Audience**: All Kotlin developers
- **Production Quality**: High (professional JetBrains production)
- **Transcript Availability**: Yes, full transcripts available
